Net proceeds from this fundraiser support the Rotary Club of Burlingame scholarship programs. Scholarships are awarded on academic achievement, community and school involvement and financial need. Three classes of scholarships are granted.

  1. Four Year College Grant Program

    Awarded to students graduating from high school and attending a 4-year college. Recipients must maintain a 3.0 GPA while in college and make annual presentations to the Rotary Club.

  2. Community College Grant Program

    Awarded to students planning to attend either their 1st or 2nd year at community college.

  3. Middle School “Rotary Scholars” Program

    Awarded to 8-10 graduates of Burlingame Intermediate School who are considered “at risk” yet capable of succeeding with extra help from Rotary mentors throughout their high school career.
